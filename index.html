ABOUT VELVET BLISS

At Velvet Bliss, every cake is handcrafted with care, elegance, and the finest ingredients. We believe every celebration deserves a beautiful centerpiece, whether it's a birthday, wedding, anniversary, baby shower, or a simple sweet treat. Using premium ingredients, we bake each cake fresh to order, creating desserts that are as delicious as they are beautiful. From timeless classics to custom creations, every cake is carefully designed to suit your special occasion. Our passion is bringing people together through unforgettable cakes that add sweetness, joy, and lasting memories—one slice at a time.
